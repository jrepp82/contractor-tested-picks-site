Why Contractor Tested Picks exists

There is no shortage of product lists written by people who have never had to depend on the gear they recommend. Contractor Tested Picks is built around a different idea: recommend products through the lens of someone who has spent decades actually working with tools, materials, equipment, fabrication processes, repairs, and field conditions.

Jason Reppen brings roughly 25 years of construction, remodeling, welding, and fabrication experience, along with Certified Welding Inspector experience. That background shapes how products are judged: function, durability, safety, value, ease of use, and whether the product solves a real problem.

And it is not only jobsite gear.

Jason also rides Harley-Davidson motorcycles and collects trading cards, so the storefront includes practical garage and Harley items plus card and collectible supplies.
