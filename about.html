About Jason

Built from real work, not a studio.

Jason Reppen is a Manitowoc, Wisconsin contractor with roughly 25 years of remodeling, construction, welding, fabrication, and inspection experience. His background includes Certified Welding Inspector work as well as hands-on residential and commercial improvement projects.

What belongs here

Tools, materials, safety gear, shop equipment, garage and Harley maintenance gear, and card-collecting supplies that solve real problems. Products Jason has personally used can be marked accordingly. Products he has not used are never presented as personal endorsements.

The goal

Help homeowners, DIYers, tradespeople, riders, and collectors spend smarter by explaining what matters, what does not, and what is actually worth paying for.
